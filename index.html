Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Case Odds

General info

Website is a WIP. Priority is to fix case odds, then later on add Souvenir Package odds and last Sticker Capsule odds.

This webpage serves the purpose of showing and visualizing Counter-Strike: Global Offensive case/capsule/package odds, in order to give the user a better overview of the actual odds of getting an item of sought rarity.

The data shown on this webpage is based on a blog post [csgo.com.cn] from 2017 by Perfect World. The same data is used for cases/capsules/packages released post-2017.

It is important to understand that probability shown under "Odds by the numbers" are the outcomes of opening an almost immeasurable sample size. For an even more accurate representation of the probability of opening a certain rarity, see "Theoretical probability." (This section doesn't exist, MIGHT be added in the future!)
